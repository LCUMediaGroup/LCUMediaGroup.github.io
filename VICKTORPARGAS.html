Vicktor Pargas

LCU Media Group Founder and CEO
Director of Original LCU short film series
LCU Film Actor

Vicktor Pargas is the original creator of the LCU as well as its conglomerate, LCU Media Group. Within the continuity of the LCU, Pargas plays a character of the same name.
Publicly, Pargas is a YouTuber turned Hollywood star known previously for his short form video game clip videos, and currently for his presence in American entertainment through his late night talk show featuring the likes of the band Weezer as guests, as well as his multitude of commercial appearances such as Pantene Conditioner. Vicktor Pargas is a bright face in Hollywood, bringing joy and cheer to Americans across the nation at home and in the cinema.
Privately however, Pargas has seen and experienced the sides of people that no one would ever hope to discover. AS PER COURT ORDER, IT MUST BE EXPRESSLY STATED THAT ALL EVENTS IN AND PERTAINING TO THE LAPTO CINEMATIC UNIVERSE AND ITS CONGLOMERATE, LCU MEDIA GROUP, ARE PURELY FICTIONAL AND THE ACTIONS OF CHARACTERS WITHIN THE MEDIA ENTITY DO NOT REFLECT THOSE OF ANY REAL PUBLIC OR PRIVATE FIGURES. Jonathan Ponce, elected Moreno Valley mayor as of January 2021, is responsible for the murder of Adam James Logan, the slaughtering and maiming of six domesticated farm animals owned by Vicktor, and the disappearance of resigned Moreno Valley mayor David Jacinto. Jonathan Ponce has evaded criminal persecution and exposing of his crimes through deep connections in local, state, and federal levels of government.
